The Book

In a garden sit the aged Kublai Khan and the young Marco Polo â€” Mongol emperor and Venetian traveler. Kublai Khan has sensed the end of his empire coming soon. Marco Polo diverts his host with stories of the cities he has seen in his travels around the empire: cities and memory, cities and desire, cities and designs, cities and the dead, cities and the sky, trading cities, hidden cities. As Marco Polo unspools his tales, the emperor detects these fantastic places are more than they appear.

The Player

This website was created to accompany you throughout your reading of the novel "The Invisible Cities" by Italo Calvino.
For each city a track is generated, all the parameters linked to it are controled by the text corresponding to its city. So as your imagination wanders trough the streets of those utopic cities their sounds will guide you trough them.
